BRAND POSITIONING

Site Intent helps people understand what their website actually
communicates.

The product analyses websites, extracts intent, compares positioning,
and recommends changes that improve clarity while preserving
differentiation.

Site Intent is: - Analytical - Intelligent - Calm - Premium -
Trustworthy - Structured

Site Intent is not: - Loud - Neon - Playful - Futuristic for the sake of
it - Generic AI purple - SEO spam energy
