SiteIntent Build Phase 5: Recommendations, Pages, and Scan History

Goal
- Deliver the main working dashboard areas that users will live in after onboarding.
- Always read `/Users/jarvis/Documents/GitHub/SiteIntent/Brand-Guidelines.txt` before building this phase.
- Make the recommendations actionable and traceable over time.

Scope
- Dashboard overview.
- Pages screen with:
  - URL
  - intent
  - contribution
  - confidence
  - updated
- Page detail view with:
  - summary
  - raw JSON
  - history
  - recommendations
- Recommendation generation limited to:
  - CHANGE
  - REMOVE
  - ADD
- Scan history with change comparison and metric deltas.

Planned Sections
- Dashboard overview
- Pages
- Recommendations
- Scan history

Deliverables
- Dashboard pages wired to real data.
- Recommendation cards or tables that stay focused on the three allowed action types.
- History views that show what changed between scans.

Acceptance Criteria
- The user can review recommendations and see where they came from.
- The pages screen makes it obvious which pages matter most.
- Scan history explains change over time instead of just showing raw runs.
