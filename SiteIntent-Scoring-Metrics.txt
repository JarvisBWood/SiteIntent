SiteIntent Scoring Metrics

SiteIntent calculates three primary scores. Each score is made up of
individual criteria, where every criterion is scored from 0 to 100 by
the AI. The application then applies the configured weightings to
calculate the final score.

The scoring engine should own the weightings so they can be adjusted
over time without changing the AI prompts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

🎯 Intent Clarity

Question: Can AI clearly understand what this business does?

  Criterion                     Weight
  --------------------------- --------
  Category clarity                 30%
  Audience clarity                 20%
  Product / Service clarity        20%
  Problem clarity                  15%
  Outcome clarity                  10%
  Messaging clarity                 5%

Each criterion is scored independently from 0 to 100.

The AI should also provide evidence explaining the score.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

🧩 Intent Consistency

Question: Do all important pages reinforce the same intent?

  Criterion                     Weight
  --------------------------- --------
  Homepage alignment               25%
  Key page alignment               35%
  Supporting page alignment        20%
  Terminology consistency          20%

Each criterion is scored independently from 0 to 100.

The AI should also provide evidence explaining the score.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

📚 Topic Coverage

Question: Has the website covered the information AI expects for this
category?

Expected topics are determined by analysing the user’s website alongside
competitors.

  Criterion                     Weight
  --------------------------- --------
  Core topics covered              45%
  Supporting topics covered        25%
  Depth of coverage                30%

Each criterion is scored independently from 0 to 100.

The AI should also provide evidence explaining the score.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

General Rules

-   Every criterion returns a score between 0 and 100.
-   The scoring engine applies the configured weightings to calculate
    the final score.
-   Every score must include supporting evidence extracted from the
    website.
-   Weightings should be configurable rather than hard-coded.
-   Only the three primary scores are displayed on the dashboard:
    -   Intent Clarity
    -   Intent Consistency
    -   Topic Coverage
